our story

GIFFY is an online secondhand clothing store that sells vintage and hand-picked pieces, delivered right to your doorstep! Rather than having to spend the time picking through loads of items at a physical thrift store, GIFFY provides convenience, quality, and style for the avid sustainable shopper. Our pieces are funky, colorful, simple, unique, and perfect for any occassion that you want to be dressed for.

We update our collection biweekly with new items, and offer great discounts and deals to our loyal customers! Our company is based in Berkeley, but ships to any address across the world to get your secondhand picks to your home in a giffy!

Our company aims to make sustainable shopping easy, fun, and affordable, so that customers can fight against fast fashion and overconsumption whilst shopping from the convenience of wherever they are. Fashion is for everyone, and we want to pave the sustainable path towards a fashionable future!
